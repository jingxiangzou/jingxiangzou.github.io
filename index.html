A Short Biography

Greetings! My name is Jingxiang Zou and I am currently a graduate student at Boston University's math finance program. You can download my resume here.

I was born in Nanchang, Jiangxi on Mar 21st, 2000. Prior to my graduate journey here at BU, I studied at Tongji University, majoring in applied mathematics and finance, from which I obtained a bachelor's degree in mathematical finance.

My past internships include roles at Haitong Securities (2023), GF Securities (2021), and China Merchant Bank (2020). During my summer internship at the Derivative Product and Trading Dept. of Haitong Securities, I conducted research related to derivative pricing and hedging. You can find more details on the 'My Work' page of this website.

Apart from my academic and professional pursuits, I enjoy teaching and have tutored students in high school math and TOEFL English. My long-term goal is to become a professor in finance and mathematical finance, and I'm actively working towards securing a Ph.D. position for the fall of 2024.

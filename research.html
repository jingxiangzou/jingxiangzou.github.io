A new ETF evalution system

    We adopt multiple measures to evaluate tracking errors based on recent data and evaluate fund liquidity as represented by volume and a special gauge of bid-ask spread proposed by Lyxor Asset Management. Based on tracking error metrics and liquidity gauges, we propose an ETF efficiency indicator that can be used to choose the optimal ETF for trading purpose

    Abstract: In the report, we adopt multiple measures to evaluate tracking errors based on recent data and evaluate fund liquidity as represented by volume and a special gauge of bid-ask spread proposed by Lyxor Asset Management.
